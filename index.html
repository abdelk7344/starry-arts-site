Reigniting Art Education

Why?

“Every child is an artist. The problem is how to remain an artist once he or she grows up.”

The lack of art education and unavailability of current art school and neighborhood programs significantly imposes substantial disadvantages on the quality of education in public high schools. Many high schools in Brooklyn and Bronx are majorly stripped from accessible resources that students can utilize in order to attain an art education. As students are restrained from having these vital resources, many of them start losing interest in the arts as they no longer remain an option for their futures.
